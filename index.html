Active Research Projects

Ongoing cutting-edge research and development work at Gopal Electronics.

AI & Healthcare

AI-enabled Non-Contact ECG using Radar Technology

Research and prototyping of contactless ECG sensing using radar (Doppler/mmWave) plus AI signal processing for beat detection, artifact removal, and arrhythmia screening.

  • Radar front-end: FMCW / MIMO mmWave options
  • Signal processing: Clutter removal, adaptive filtering
  • AI Models: Deep learning for waveform extraction
Assistive Tech

Smart Wheelchair

Integrated smart wheelchair platform supporting mobile app control, voice/audio commands, gyroscope-based stabilization, joystick fallback, and AI-driven assistance.

  • Controls: Mobile app (BLE/Wi‑Fi), Joystick, Voice
  • Sensors: Gyroscope, IMU, obstacle detection
  • AI: Path-planning, user intent recognition
Power Electronics

High-Frequency Sine Wave Inverters

Design and testing of compact, high-frequency inverters optimized for low-harmonic distortion and high-efficiency power conversion.

  • Topologies: LLC, full-bridge with advanced modulation
  • Control: DSP/FPGA-based control loops
  • Applications: UPS, renewable integration
Solar Systems

MPPT & Solar Charge Controllers

Maximum Power Point Tracking algorithms and robust solar charge controller firmware and hardware with battery management.

  • MPPT Methods: Perturb & Observe, AI-assisted
  • Features: Multi-stage charging, telemetry
  • Form Factors: Standalone, hybrid-integrated
Wireless Audio

Contactless Audio Amplification (NFA)

Developing a 'lay-on' speaker system that captures and amplifies audio from a mobile device using near-field magnetic induction, eliminating the need for pairing or cables.

  • Sensor Tech: High-sensitivity inductive pickup coils
  • Signal Processing: Noise cancellation algorithms
  • Hardware: Compact, power-efficient amplifier circuits
Adversarial Research

Preventing Magnetic Eavesdropping on Audio Devices

This project focuses on preventing the unauthorized capture of audio signals from wireless devices like TWS earbuds. We are developing proprietary shielding and counter-measure technologies to defend against magnetic side-channel attacks, ensuring user privacy and preventing the misuse of sensitive information.

  • Analysis: Characterizing magnetic attack surfaces
  • Defense: Developing authentication protocols
  • Implementation: Hardware & firmware countermeasures
